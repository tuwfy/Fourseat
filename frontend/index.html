How it works

How Fourseat works
step by step

Fourseat runs four role-based analyses in parallel, then combines them into one board-style decision summary you can act on quickly.

1. Ask your question
Enter a decision, problem, or strategy question with optional context — stage, metrics, constraints.
2. Four perspectives
Strategy, finance, technology, and a contrarian respond independently so blind spots are surfaced early.
3. A clear verdict
A final synthesis returns a verdict, key risks, opportunities, and concrete action steps.
Other modules

Fourseat Memory: Upload documents and search past decisions in plain English.
Fourseat Decks: Enter the key data and generate a board-ready presentation draft.

what it is and is not
