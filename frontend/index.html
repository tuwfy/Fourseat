How it works

How Fourseat works
step by step

Fourseat runs four role-based analyses in parallel, then combines them into one board-style decision summary you can act on quickly.

Oracle plugs into your operational stack, watches it continuously, and produces a board-ready verdict the moment something matters. Four steps, fully automated.

1. Connect Stripe
One API key (or signed webhook) connects your revenue stack. Oracle backfills 90 days and starts watching every event.
2. Detect anomalies
Six rules run on every snapshot: churn cluster, NRR drop, failed-payment leakage, concentration risk, expansion stall, tier collapse.
3. Convene the board
Strategy, Finance, Technology, and a Contrarian each weigh in. The Chair compresses the debate into one verdict and a confidence score.
4. Ship the deck
One click to generate a board-ready PowerPoint with the verdict, evidence, advisor panel, recommended actions, and watch metrics.
