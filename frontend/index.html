How Fourseat works
step by step

Oracle connects your entire operational stack into a single AI layer. Five sources, one closed loop. Every signal compared, every anomaly surfaced, every verdict actionable.

1. Connect everything
One-click OAuth for Stripe, QuickBooks, GitHub, Linear, and Slack. Oracle backfills 90 days of history from each source and begins real-time monitoring.
2. Cross-source detection
Rules run across all sources: revenue vs. engineering output, sprint velocity vs. churn, Slack sentiment vs. financial health. Blind spots other tools miss.
3. Convene the board
Strategy, Finance, Tech, and a Contrarian advisor each reason across the full context. The Chair synthesizes one verdict you can act on immediately.
4. Close the loop
Every resolution feeds back into the system. Oracle learns which signals matter to your company and gets sharper with every cycle. Open loop becomes closed loop.
