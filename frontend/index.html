How it works

How Fourseat works
step by step

Fourseat runs four role-based analyses in parallel, then combines them into one board-style decision summary you can act on quickly.

1) Ask your question
Enter a decision, problem, or strategy question with optional context (stage, metrics, constraints).
2) Four role perspectives
You get Strategy, Finance, Technology, and Contrarian views so blind spots are surfaced early.
3) Board Chair decision
A final synthesis returns verdict, risks, opportunities, and concrete action steps.
Other modules

Fourseat Memory: Upload docs and query historical decisions.

About Fourseat
what it is and is not

Outputs are guidance, not legal, tax, or investment advice. Use your own judgment and qualified professional advisors when needed.
