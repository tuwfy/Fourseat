# Fourseat Launch Day Playbook

Use this checklist to launch Fourseat across Product Hunt, Hacker News, Indie Hackers, AppSumo, and G2/Capterra with consistent positioning and clean execution.

## 1) Core positioning (use everywhere)

- **One-liner:** Fourseat gives founders an AI board of directors that debates decisions, remembers company context, and generates board briefs.
- **Primary audience:** startup founders, solo builders, small SaaS teams, operators.
- **Main outcomes:** faster decisions, less context switching, clearer board-level communication.

## 2) Launch assets checklist (must-have before posting)

- Landing page with one clear CTA (`Join waitlist` or `Start trial`)
- Product demo video (60-90 sec) showing 3 key workflows:
  - Ask the boardroom a decision question
  - Retrieve company context from BoardMind
  - Generate a board brief/deck
- 6-8 product screenshots (desktop + mobile web if relevant)
- Founder profile + company description
- Pricing page or waitlist explanation
- Support contact email and privacy/terms links
- Analytics + conversion tracking enabled

## 3) Shared proof points to include in every post

- Multi-AI boardroom debate with final recommendation
- Persistent company memory over uploaded docs
- Instant board-ready brief generation
- Built for founder decision speed and clarity

## 4) Channel-by-channel launch kit

## Product Hunt

### Setup

- Reserve launch date and prep product page 3-5 days early
- Add logo, tagline, gallery images, and first comment draft
- Prepare founder comments for first 6-8 hours

### Copy starter

- **Tagline:** Your AI board of directors for founder decisions
- **Description:** Fourseat runs a multi-AI boardroom debate on your toughest decisions, pulls context from your company docs, and generates board-ready briefs in minutes.
- **First comment:** We built Fourseat to help founders make high-stakes decisions faster. Ask your question, get multiple AI perspectives, and leave with a clear recommendation and briefing.

### Day-one execution

- Launch in the morning (PT)
- Ask close users/customers to engage in first 2-3 hours
- Reply to every comment quickly with concrete examples

## Hacker News (Show HN)

### Setup

- Post only after testing onboarding and demo flow end-to-end
- Keep title direct and non-marketing

### Post title options

- Show HN: Fourseat, an AI board of directors for startup founders
- Show HN: We built a multi-AI boardroom for founder decisions

### Post body starter

Built Fourseat to reduce founder decision paralysis. You can ask a high-stakes question, get a multi-AI debate, pull context from your uploaded docs, and generate a board-ready brief. Looking for blunt feedback on usefulness, UX, and what feels missing.

### Day-one execution

- Stay in thread for at least 6 hours
- Answer criticisms with specifics, not hype
- Ship quick fixes and post updates in-thread

## Indie Hackers

### Setup

- Publish a launch story (problem, build process, early lessons)
- Share pricing/waitlist model transparently

### Post structure

- Problem founders face (decision fatigue, fragmented context)
- Why existing tools fail for board-level decision quality
- What Fourseat does differently
- Ask for specific feedback (pricing, onboarding, missing feature)

### CTA

- Invite founders to try and share one real decision they used it for

## AppSumo

### Setup

- Define lifetime-deal packaging before listing:
  - Tier 1: solo founder
  - Tier 2: small team
  - Tier 3: agency/operator bundle
- Prepare redemption, onboarding, and support workflow

### Listing essentials

- Benefit-focused headline
- Clear limits per tier (credits/seats/uploads)
- FAQ addressing stacking, refunds, roadmap, and support SLA

### Day-one execution

- Monitor Q&A hourly for first 48 hours
- Publish public roadmap to build trust with deal buyers

## G2 and Capterra

### Setup

- Create listings with consistent category positioning:
  - AI productivity / Decision support / Knowledge management
- Add high-quality screenshots + short explainer video
- Fill every profile field (missing fields reduce conversion)

### Review engine

- Ask first 10-20 users for honest reviews
- Use review request email after successful first value moment
- Never incentivize with prohibited tactics

### SEO notes

- Repeat core phrase naturally in listing copy:
  - AI board of directors for founders
- Keep feature bullets concrete and outcome-driven

## 5) Launch week timeline

- **T-7 to T-3:** finalize assets, analytics, email flows, onboarding
- **T-2 to T-1:** preload Product Hunt + G2/Capterra profiles, draft HN and IH posts
- **Launch day:** Product Hunt + Hacker News first, then Indie Hackers
- **T+1 to T+3:** AppSumo listing push, user interviews, FAQ updates
- **T+4 to T+7:** collect testimonials, request reviews, ship top 3 onboarding fixes

## 6) Metrics to track from day one

- Visitors by channel
- Waitlist/trial conversion rate per channel
- Time-to-first-value (minutes to first useful output)
- Activation rate (completed first debate + memory query + brief)
- Cost per signup and payback signal by channel

## 7) Reusable launch copy blocks

### Short pitch (X/LinkedIn)

Fourseat is an AI board of directors for founders. Ask a high-stakes question, get a multi-AI debate, pull context from your company docs, and generate a board-ready brief in minutes.

### Cold outreach line

If you had an AI boardroom that gave you a final recommendation on your next big decision, what would you ask first?

### Review request email (G2/Capterra)

Subject: Quick favor? Share your Fourseat experience

Hi {{name}},

Thanks again for trying Fourseat. If it has helped your decision workflow, would you mind leaving a short honest review? It helps other founders find us and tells us what to improve next.

{{review_link}}

Thanks,
Fourseat team

## 8) Final go/no-go preflight

- Demo works from clean browser session
- No broken links on homepage, legal, pricing, contact
- Waitlist/trial flow tested with real email
- Analytics events firing for CTA and signup completion
- One team member assigned to each channel during launch window

If all boxes are checked, launch.
